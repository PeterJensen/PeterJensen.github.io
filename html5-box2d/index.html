Box2D with SIMD in JavaScript

Ningxin Hu and Peter Jensen

Here you'll find the presentation and other relevant material we presented at the October, 2014 HTML5 Developer Conference.

In order to get the best experience for the SIMD enabled demos you must use one of the Firefox Nightly builds, or a special experimental Intel build of Chromium with SIMD.

The links to download these are below. Once installed, launch it and reopen this web page.
